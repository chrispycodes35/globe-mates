# GlobeMates

> A platform helping international students transition, connect, and thrive abroad.

---

**GlobeMates** helps study-abroad students navigate new environments, build meaningful connections, and access the right resources through their host universities.

---

**GlobeMates** provides a centralized, human-centered platform for international students to:
- 🤝 **Connect with peers and mentors** who share similar interests or backgrounds  
- 🧭 **Access verified resources** from host universities and cultural offices  
- 🎉 **Join cultural circles** and local community events  
- 📱 **Receive personalized guidance** based on location and stage of study  


---

## ⚙️ How It Works
1. **Sign up** using your university email.  
2. **Complete your profile** with interests and location.  
3. **Get matched** with peers and mentors.  
4. **Discover events**, resources, and local opportunities.  

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** React / Next.js  
- **Backend:** Node.js + Express  
- **Database:** Firebase  
- **Integrations:** Google Maps API, University APIs  
- **Design Tools:** Figma, Miro
